== Description ==

Shadow Fade is a sophisticated dark block theme featuring elegant shadows and minimalist aesthetics. Designed for content creators and photographers who prefer dark interfaces, it reduces eye strain while maintaining excellent readability through carefully balanced contrast and premium Inter/Outfit typography. Perfect for blogs, portfolios, and professional websites, it offers clean layouts and complete customization through full site editing capabilities.
